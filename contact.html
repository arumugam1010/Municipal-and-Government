Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries regarding registration, taxes, and service requests.

How do I register a new account on the portal?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. Select your portal workspace role (Citizen, Officer, or Admin), enter your full name and email address, and complete registration.

What is the average response time for complaints?

Grievances filed via the Public Complaints dashboard are routed to municipal officers instantly. Most issues are evaluated within 24 hours, and resolutions are coordinated within 48 to 72 hours.

Can I download e-signed certificates directly from my dashboard?

Yes! Once a certificate application (such as Birth or Marriage certificate) is approved and e-signed by municipal officers, you can download the PDF file directly from the "Certificates" tab in your citizen workspace.
